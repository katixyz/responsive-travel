Who we are and what we do

We are Kati, 30 years old environmental geographer and Milo, 3 years old medium poodle. We want to share the experiences from all our adventures and to document our common path. Usually, we are travelling with a camper van or staying at dog-friendly Airbnb's. We travel all over Europe and want to see as many countries together as possible.

The most important thing to me is that I want you to know that I always report from my point of view and you should always consider what your dog is like and what's best for you. Even though I always try to assess which kind of dogs would have fun at the different travels or activities, in the end it is always your decision to make. Hopefully, you can take away a lot of valuable tips for your next trip or travel. Please, never hesitate to get in touch with us if you have any questions or different experiences. We love to get in touch and exchange stories with you!
